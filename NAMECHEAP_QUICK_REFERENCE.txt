TTL: Automatic
(NO QUOTES - Namecheap adds them!)

## Important
- Click ✓ (checkmark) after EACH record to save
- Do NOT include .suopay.io in Host field
- Do NOT add quotes to TXT values
- Wait 15 mins - 48 hours for DNS propagation
