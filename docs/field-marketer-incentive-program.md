### Active Status Definition

A marketer is considered **ACTIVE** if they:
- Achieve minimum 10 Pro subscribers in a 5-day cycle
- Have been active for at least 2 of the last 4 weeks
- Respond to VA check-ins within 24 hours

A marketer becomes **INACTIVE** if they:
- Miss target for 2 consecutive weeks
- Fail to report daily progress
- Have no verified sales for 7+ days

## FAQ

### For Marketers

**Q: How do I become a Supervisor?**
A: Recruit 5 active marketers who each hit their 10/week target for 2 consecutive weeks, while maintaining your own 5/week sales.

**Q: Do I lose my direct commission if I become a Supervisor?**
A: No! You keep earning â‚¦500 for your own sales PLUS â‚¦50 for each sale your team makes.

**Q: What if my recruit doesn't perform?**
A: You only earn override on actual sales. If they sell 0, you earn â‚¦0 override from them. This is why you should recruit motivated people and help them succeed.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many people I can recruit?**
A: No limit, but you only earn override on your DIRECT recruits (L1). If they become Supervisors, you earn L2 override from their teams.

### For Supervisors

**Q: How do I track my team's sales?**
A: The VA will send you a daily team summary. You can also request the weekly sheet at any time.

**Q: What happens if one of my marketers becomes a Supervisor?**
A: Congratulations! You automatically become eligible for L2 status. You'll earn â‚¦25 for every sale their team makes.

**Q: Can I recruit from other Supervisor's teams?**
A: No. Once a marketer is assigned to a team, they cannot be transferred. You must recruit new people.

### For VA/Admin

**Q: How do I verify sales?**
A: Open SuoOps Admin â†’ Referral Program â†’ Filter by referral code or date. The dashboard is the source of truth.

**Q: What if there's a dispute?**
A: The referral code entered by the customer is recorded in the system. If the code wasn't entered, the sale doesn't count for that marketer.

**Q: How do I calculate complex scenarios?**
A: Use the Commission Calculator tab for stipends and overrides. Direct commissions are handled automatically by the app.

**Q: What if customer forgot to enter referral code?**
A: Unfortunately, that sale won't be credited. Train marketers to ALWAYS ensure the code is entered during signup. They can watch the customer enter it.
